Q: A carpenter bought a piece of wood that was 0.41 meters long . Then she sawed 0.33 meters off the end . How long is the piece of wood now ?
A: The question can be simplified to: It is known that A = 0.41, B = 0.33. Subtract B from A, now what is the value of A?
A = A - B = 0.41 - 0.33 = 0.08
So the answer is: 0.08
